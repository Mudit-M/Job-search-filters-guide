Career Search Automation

2026 Restructuring Support Edition | Speed & Precision

Note for Colleagues: Speed is the #1 factor in a tight market. This guide helps you set up a "hands-off" system that finds jobs the minute they are posted.

1 Google Alert (ATS Tracker)

Catches new posts on internal career sites like Greenhouse, Lever, and Workday.

("Agentforce" OR "Data Cloud" OR "Data 360" OR "CRM Analytics" OR "CRMA") AND (site:greenhouse.io OR site:lever.co OR site:jobs.ashbyhq.com OR site:myworkdayjobs.com OR site:smartrecruiters.com OR site:jobs.icims.com) -inurl:blog -inurl:news

Set Alert to: As-it-happens | All Results

2 LinkedIn Specialized Alert

Essential because LinkedIn roles often don't show up in Google Alerts.

("Agentforce" OR "Data Cloud" OR "Data 360" OR "CRM Analytics" OR "CRMA") AND ("Architect" OR "Consultant" OR "Solution Architect")

Search in the Jobs tab and toggle "Set Alert" to ON.

3 The "Daily Sweep" Bookmark

The most powerful tool. A manual search link for roles posted in the last 24 hours only.

Pro-Tips

  • Refresh the indexing: If the sweep link feels stale, change the URL end from qdr:d to qdr:h to see the past hour only.
  • Referrals: Found a role on a Lever/Greenhouse link? Check LinkedIn for a mutual connection first.
