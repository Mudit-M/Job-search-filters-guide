🚀 Job Search Automation

In the current market, speed and precision are our best tools. This guide helps you automate your search for high-demand roles (Data 360, Agentforce, Analytics) using Google Alerts and Advanced Search Logic.

1. Understanding the Logic

1. Simple Search: ("Agentforce" OR "Data Cloud")
Broad but noisy; returns news and blogs.
2. Role Precision: Adds AND ("Architect" OR "Consultant")
Ensures tech stack matches a job title.
3. X-Ray Filter: Adds site:lever.co
Forces Google to only look at Applicant Tracking Systems.
4. Negative Filter: Adds -inurl:blog
Strips away articles and press releases.

2. The 3-Step Strategy

Step 1: Google Alert (24/7 Scout)

Emails you the moment a company career page is updated.

("Agentforce" OR "Data Cloud" OR "Data 360" OR "CRM Analytics" OR "CRMA") AND (site:greenhouse.io OR site:lever.co OR site:jobs.ashbyhq.com OR site:myworkdayjobs.com OR site:smartrecruiters.com OR site:jobs.icims.com) -inurl:blog -inurl:news

Set to: As-it-happens | All Results

Step 2: LinkedIn Specialized Alert

Note: LinkedIn pages are dynamic; you must set this alert directly in the LinkedIn Jobs tab.

("Agentforce" OR "Data Cloud" OR "Data 360" OR "CRM Analytics" OR "CRMA") AND ("Architect" OR "Consultant" OR "Solution Architect")
